Research Projects Attempted
Face Reconstruction for Video Chatting Modelling of Simultaneously Multi-Threaded Processors

Personal Project

2013

The idea was to extract and transmit prior information about a face to the other party so that, during the chat, only a small bitrate is required to reconstruct the face. I acheived surprisingly decent reconstructions using only PCA (with a frontal face) and 120 bytes per frame (uncompressed). Further studies are required to incorporate the less significant eigenvectors and compensate for race rotation.

Prof. V. Rajaraman, IISc Bangalore
